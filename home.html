ABOUT US | OUR STORY

“Every sip is a quiet gesture of kindness.”

Moonbucks was born from a shared love of coffee and an age-old tale that still stirs hearts. Inspired by the East Asian legend of the Moon Rabbit — a mythical figure said to craft elixirs on the moon — we envisioned a café that brews more than just coffee.

Like the rabbit’s quiet devotion, every cup we serve is made with intention, warmth, and care. To us, coffee is a ritual — a chance to pause, breathe, and find a little peace in the middle of it all.

At Moonbucks, we don’t just serve drinks. We serve comfort, good fortune, and a touch of lunar magic in every sip.
